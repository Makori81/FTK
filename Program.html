Feeding Programs

Our feeding programs are designed to ensure that every child receives the nutrition they need to thrive.

Daily Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Porridge, whole wheat bread
  • Mid-morning Snack: Fresh fruit
  • Lunch: Rice, beans, vegetable salad
  • Afternoon Snack: Cocoa, Biscuits
  • Dinner: Ugali, greens, beef, fresh fruit

Weekly Menu Rotation

Tuesday Ugali and sukuma wiki Lentil soup with rice

Special Events

  • Monthly Community Cookout: Engage local volunteers to cook and serve meals, fostering community spirit.
  • Nutritional Education Workshops: Monthly sessions to educate children and caregivers about healthy eating habits.
  • Festive Meals: Special menus on holidays and birthdays to celebrate with the children.

Volunteers & Support

  • Volunteer Chefs: Local chefs can volunteer their time to cook meals and teach the children basic cooking skills.
  • Donor Support: Engage donors and sponsors to provide ingredients and financial support.
  • Community Gardens: Develop a small garden at the orphanage to grow fresh vegetables and fruits.
